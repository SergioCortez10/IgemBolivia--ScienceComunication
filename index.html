Content and statistics

Bolivia has a major issue with science communication and therefore no science culture among citizens in general. This was our main reason to start different activities that would engage and inform the public in general about biotechnology and synthetic biology. Here we present a summary of the different activities carried out by the iGEM Bolivia team.

Content

This was our core activity ever since we formed the group in 2020. During quarantine, we started understanding how social networks operate and finally we built a publications calendar. First the topics were random but around a year ago, we work with a defined topic per month. We have covered from basic biotechnology concepts to specific ones about our project: biotechnology classification, microorganisms, bioreactors, biosensors and even a period talking about vaccines to educate our public given the situation.

PODCAST: CONCIENCIA

What is CONCIENCIA?

As members of iGEM Bolivia in the field of Science Communication, we created around October 2020 the Podcast ConCiencia. Our goal is to familiarize people with scientific research, genetic engineering and biotechnology. We discovered that there was a problem: scientific podcasts are not very popular in Bolivia. Our target audience is public in general but especially young people, so we found that the best way to achieve our main goal is to share authentic experiences of inspiring Bolivian professionals and students related to the biotechnology fields. Until now, we have more than a dozen interviews published and other special episodes. We are very satisfied that this project has acquired an international impact, having reproductions in different countries. With this initiative, we have been able to develop leadership, collaboration and communication skills, while creating a close-knit team. We are currently working on our second season with the mid-term goals of expanding to Latin America and becoming one of the leading Biotechnology podcasts in Bolivia.

Impact

ConCiencia Podcast with the aim of reaching the general public and generate interest in the area of biotechnology was well received not only in Bolivia, but also in other countries such as: Chile, United States, Belgium, Portugal and Mexico. We can define this as the beginning of a journey in which Bolivia is being taken into account as a country that knows about biotechnology and has biotechnologists around the world as reflected in some of our podcasts. Therefore, we are very motivated to continue with this work.

COViGEM

The iGEM Bolivia team is committed to scientists and the population in general with regard to the dissemination of science-related topics of impact. For that reason we have opened this new space, the cure for misinformation, CoviGEM Bolivia 🇧🇴 in which we will share updated scientific information on COVID-19 topics.

NutriGEM

NutriGEM aims to create informative content for social networks, on topics, news and research on food and science. NutriGEM is a prototype of entrepreneurship formed by our team that seeks to offer a nutritious alternative to the public.

Dissemination in the press

We were able to manage the communication of our progress in different media in order to publicize the work being done by the team and invite the population to support us. The advantage of reaching mass media such as television and newspapers is that we were able to reach a wider audience. Last year we had the opportunity to be interviewed on the television program "Jaque Mate" of the university channel of the Universidad Mayor de San Andrés (UMSA) - TVU, because we won the 3rd prize in the university fair "INVESTIGA UMSA 2020", where we presented our project as "Development of an arsenic biosensor through synthetic biology". Another opportunity we had, was to publish 4 articles in the magazine "LIBRE EMPRESA" on various topics related to biotechnology.
